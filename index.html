Priority 01 — Medi-Cal Defense

Protecting the coverage 15 million Californians depend on

Medi-Cal is the backbone of California's healthcare safety net. As federal Medicaid cuts are proposed in Washington, we need a governor who will go to the mat to protect Medi-Cal enrollees — especially low-income families, seniors, people with disabilities, and undocumented immigrants who have nowhere else to turn.

15M Californians enrolled in Medi-Cal
Priority 02 — Drug Pricing & CalRx

Making medications affordable for every Californian

As HHS Secretary, Becerra fought pharmaceutical companies on insulin prices. As Governor, he will expand CalRx — California's generic drug initiative — and use the state's purchasing power to negotiate lower prices on the medications patients actually need.

$35 Insulin price cap already secured
Priority 03 — Reproductive Healthcare

Ensuring every woman receives the care she needs

California must remain a safe haven for reproductive healthcare. Becerra will defend existing protections, ensure abortion access statewide, and fight against federal attempts to restrict reproductive rights in our state.

Priorities 04–06 — Workforce, Telehealth & Disaster Prep

Building a resilient healthcare system for the future

California faces a physician shortage crisis — especially in rural and underserved communities. Becerra's plan expands loan forgiveness, telehealth infrastructure, and community health worker pipelines. And after the LA fires, disaster-ready hospitals and supply chains are non-negotiable.

About Xavier Becerra

A proven fighter
for patient rights.

Xavier Becerra spent a decade fighting pharmaceutical companies and insurance corporations as California's Attorney General. As President Biden's HHS Secretary, he expanded the ACA, negotiated lower drug prices, and defended Medicaid against repeated attacks. He knows our system from the inside — and he knows where it's broken.

🛡️
Defend Medi-Cal at all costs
Fight federal cuts, maintain eligibility, and expand access for all Californians who qualify.
💊
Expand CalRx statewide

Grow the healthcare workforce
